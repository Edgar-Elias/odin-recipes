Grilled Cheese


The world's most delicious Italian lasagna

With a little work, a little love and small amount of ingredients you can make this dish possible!

This recipe accomodates for about 12 people or 12 servings. The cook time is aproximately 2hrs 30min with with an aditional time of 15 min for cooling. Preparation of dish takes about 30 min. Total time approximately 3 hrs 15 min. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of fresh sourdough bread
  • 1 tbs of butter
  • 3 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbs of mayonnaise

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat up a pan to medium or medium low heat.
  2. Butter each slice of bread.
  3. On the non butter side of bread spread mayonnaise.
  4. Place slice of cheese between butter sides of bread.
  5. Place sandwich on hot pan with mayonnaise side down.
  6. Cook sandwich about 3 min using a spatula to flip each side until golden brown and cheese melted.
  7. Remove from pan and enjoy!

Grilled Cheese


My world's most delicious Grilled Cheese

With mall amount of ingredients you can make this dish possible!

This recipe serves 1 person. The cook time is aproximately 15min. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of fresh sourdough bread
  • 1 tbs of butter
  • 3 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbs of mayonnaise

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat up a pan to medium or medium low heat.
  2. Butter each slice of bread.
  3. On the non butter side of bread spread mayonnaise.
  4. Place slice of cheese between butter sides of bread.
  5. Place sandwich on hot pan with mayonnaise side down.
  6. Cook sandwich about 3 min using a spatula to flip each side until golden brown and cheese melted.
  7. Remove from pan and enjoy!

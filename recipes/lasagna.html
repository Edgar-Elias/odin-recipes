Lasagna

The world's most delicious Italian lasagna

With a little work, a little love and small amount of ingredients you can make this dish possible!

This recipe accomodates for about 12 people or 12 servings. The cook time is aproximately 2hrs 30min with with an aditional time of 15 min for cooling. Preparation of dish takes about 30 min. Total time approximately 3 hrs 15 min. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can of Ragú Traditional Sauce
  • 12 sheets of lasagna noodles
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pound Mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 pound Riccotta cheese
  • 3/4 pound Parmasean cheese
  • 4 tbs chopped fresh parsely
  • 4 tbs chopped fresh basil
  • 3 dried bay leafs
  • 1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 freshly crushed black pepper

Recipe Instructions

  1. In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stirring occasionally.
  2. Once meat is browned, add can of Ragú along with 3 dried bay leafs and bring to a boil.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine Ricotta cheese with egg, 3 tbs parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. Spread 1 1/2 cups of Ragú sauce in the bottom of a baking dish (preferably 9 by 12 - inch) used for lasagna. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat steps for following layers, and top off with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil making sure the foil does not touch the cheese to prevent sticking.
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Remove from oven and let cool for 15 minutes
